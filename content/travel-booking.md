## Card: What it is
image: travelBookingHero
**Booking flights and accommodation** is where much of a trip's cost is decided. Prices vary enormously based on timing, flexibility, and how you book. Knowing a few principles helps you avoid overpaying and dodge booking pitfalls. (General travel guidance.)

## Card: Why it matters
Flights and accommodation are usually the biggest travel expenses, and the difference between a smart and careless booking can be substantial. Understanding how pricing works helps you save money and avoid common traps like hidden fees and non-refundable surprises. It directly affects how far your travel budget stretches.

## Card: Key terms
- **Flexibility**: how much your dates/times can vary.
- **Hidden fees**: extra charges added beyond the base price.
- **Refundable vs. non-refundable**: whether you can cancel and get money back.
- **Comparison sites**: tools to compare prices across providers.

## Card: Save on flights
Flight prices swing with demand and timing. General money-savers: book in advance (but not always too early), be **flexible** with dates and times (mid-week and off-peak flights are often cheaper), and use **comparison sites** to check across airlines. Watch for **hidden fees** — budget airlines especially add charges for baggage, seats, and more, so compare the *total* price, not just the headline fare.

## Card: Booking accommodation wisely
image: accommodationChoice
For accommodation: compare options on review and **comparison sites**, read recent reviews carefully (not just the rating), and check the location actually suits your plans. Note the cancellation policy — **refundable** bookings cost a bit more but offer flexibility if plans change, while **non-refundable** ones are cheaper but risky. Watch for added fees (cleaning, resort, city taxes) that inflate the final price beyond the listed rate.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: comparing only headline fares while ignoring hidden baggage/seat fees; booking non-refundable without considering the risk; trusting star ratings over actual recent reviews; not checking the location; and booking the first option without comparing. Compare total prices, read reviews, mind cancellation terms, and watch for fees.

## Card: Quick recap
Flights and accommodation set much of a trip's cost. Save by booking ahead, staying flexible with dates, using comparison sites, and comparing *total* prices (watch hidden baggage and added fees). For stays, read recent reviews, check the location, and mind refundable-versus-non-refundable terms. Smart booking stretches your travel budget significantly.
