## Card: What it is
image: growthFocusUnderstandingAttentionHero
**Attention** is your mind's ability to focus on one thing while ignoring others. It's a limited resource — you can't truly focus on everything at once. In a world designed to grab it, understanding how attention works is the first step to taking back control of your own mind.

## Card: Why it matters
Your attention shapes your experience: what you focus on is, in a real sense, what your life consists of. It's also under constant assault from apps and notifications engineered to capture it. Understanding attention helps you protect it, do better work, and be more present — a genuinely valuable modern skill.

## Card: Key terms
- **Attention**: focusing the mind on one thing, filtering out others.
- **Selective attention**: choosing what to focus on.
- **Attention span**: how long you can sustain focus.
- **Attention economy**: businesses competing to capture your focus.

## Card: Attention is limited
image: growthFocusUnderstandingAttentionAttentionIsLimitedHero
Your mind has a limited pool of focus. **Selective attention** means concentrating on one thing necessarily filters out others — you can't fully attend to many things at once. This is why a noisy, notification-filled environment is so draining: your attention is constantly being split and pulled, leaving little for what you actually intend to focus on.

## Card: The battle for your focus
We live in an **attention economy**: countless apps and platforms profit by capturing your attention and holding it as long as possible. They're expertly designed to be hard to put down. This isn't a personal failing — you're up against teams engineering for your distraction. Recognizing that is the first step to defending your focus.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: treating attention as unlimited and trying to focus on everything; blaming yourself entirely for distraction when apps are engineered to grab you; underestimating how draining constant attention-splitting is; and not protecting attention as the precious, limited resource it is. Awareness of the battle is the start of winning it.

## Card: Quick recap
Attention is your limited ability to focus on one thing while filtering out others — and it shapes your experience of life. It's under constant assault in the attention economy, where apps are engineered to capture it. This isn't a personal failing; recognizing the battle is the first step to protecting your focus.
