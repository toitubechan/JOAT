## Card: What it is
image: usefulPhoneFeaturesHero
Modern phones come packed with genuinely useful **built-in features** that many people never discover — beyond calls, texts, and a few apps. Knowing they exist means you can use the powerful tool in your pocket to its potential, often replacing several separate gadgets and apps.

## Card: Why it matters
Most people use a small fraction of what their phone can do. A few of these features save real time, money, or hassle — from navigation to accessibility tools that make phones easier for everyone. Discovering them turns your phone from a communication device into a versatile everyday assistant.

## Card: Key terms
- **Accessibility settings**: features making phones easier to use.
- **Voice assistant**: a built-in helper you can talk to.
- **Maps / navigation**: turn-by-turn directions.
- **Digital wallet**: storing cards to pay with your phone.

## Card: Everyday power tools
A few standouts: **maps and navigation** give free turn-by-turn directions and public transport times; the **camera** doubles as a scanner and even a translator for text; a **voice assistant** sets reminders, alarms, and answers questions hands-free; and a **digital wallet** lets you pay by tapping your phone instead of carrying cards.

## Card: Accessibility for everyone
image: accessibilitySettings
**Accessibility settings** aren't only for people with disabilities — they help everyone. You can enlarge text, increase contrast, have the phone read text aloud, use voice control, or magnify the screen. If your phone ever feels hard to read or use, these settings can make it dramatically more comfortable.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: paying for apps or gadgets your phone already replaces (torch, scanner, sat-nav); never exploring the **accessibility settings** that could make the phone easier to use; and not trying the **voice assistant** for quick hands-free tasks. Many of the best features are free and already installed — just undiscovered.

## Card: Quick recap
Phones include powerful free features many people miss: navigation, a camera that scans and translates, a voice assistant for hands-free tasks, and a digital wallet for tap payments. Accessibility settings make phones easier for everyone. Explore what's built in — you may already own tools you've been buying separately.
