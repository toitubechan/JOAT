## Card: What it is
image: financePersonalMoneyGoalsHero
A **money goal** is a specific thing you're saving or planning toward — not just "have more money," but "save €1,000 for a trip by next summer." Goals turn a vague wish into a clear target with an amount and a deadline, which makes it something you can actually work toward.

## Card: Why it matters
Without goals, money tends to just... disappear. A clear goal gives every spending choice a reason: each time you skip an impulse buy, you're moving toward something you actually want. Goals make saving feel purposeful instead of like punishment.

## Card: Key terms
- **Short-term goal**: reachable within about a year.
- **Medium-term goal**: roughly one to five years away.
- **Long-term goal**: five-plus years, like retirement.
- **SMART goal**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound.

## Card: Three time horizons
image: financePersonalMoneyGoalsThreeTimeHorizonsHero
Spread your goals across timeframes. **Short-term** goals (a new laptop, a holiday) keep you motivated with quick wins. **Medium-term** goals (a car, a course) need steady saving. **Long-term** goals (a home deposit, retirement) seem distant but benefit most from starting early, thanks to compounding growth.

## Card: Make goals SMART
A vague goal fails quietly. A **SMART** goal is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. Compare "save more" with "save €200 a month for 6 months for an emergency fund." The second tells you exactly what to do, lets you track progress, and tells you when you've succeeded.

## Card: Common mistakes
The first mistake is setting only one giant, distant goal — progress feels invisible and motivation fades. Break big goals into monthly milestones. The second is setting goals with no deadline; an open-ended goal is easy to postpone forever. A date creates gentle pressure to act.

## Card: Quick recap
Money goals turn wishes into targets with an amount and a deadline. Mix short-, medium-, and long-term goals, make each one SMART, and break big ones into monthly steps. Clear goals are what give your saving direction and your spending a purpose.
