## Card: What it is
image: appsStorageHero
**Apps** are the programs that give your phone its abilities, and **storage** is the limited space where they and your files live. Managing both — installing safely, organizing sensibly, and clearing space — keeps your phone useful, tidy, and running smoothly.

## Card: Why it matters
A cluttered phone with no free storage runs slowly, can't update, and won't take photos. Knowing how to install apps safely protects you from harmful software, and managing storage prevents the frustrating "storage full" wall. These are everyday skills that keep your phone working as it should.

## Card: Key terms
- **App store**: the official place to download apps safely.
- **Storage**: the space for apps, photos, and files.
- **Cache**: temporary files apps build up over time.
- **Uninstall**: removing an app you no longer need.

## Card: Installing apps safely
Download apps only from the official **app store** for your phone (Apple's App Store or Google Play). These check apps for safety, unlike random websites or links, which can carry harmful software. Before installing, glance at reviews and the **permissions** it requests — be wary of an app wanting access it has no reason to need.

## Card: Freeing up storage
image: clearPhoneStorage
When **storage** fills up, a few moves help: **uninstall** apps you don't use; delete or back up old photos and videos (often the biggest space-eaters); and clear app **cache** (temporary files). Many phones have a "storage" screen in settings that shows what's using space and suggests what to remove.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: installing apps from outside the official store, risking malware; granting apps unnecessary permissions; letting photos and unused apps pile up until storage is full; and panicking at "storage full" instead of knowing the simple fixes. A quick periodic tidy keeps the phone responsive and ready.

## Card: Quick recap
Apps give your phone its powers; storage is the limited space they share. Install only from the official app store, check permissions, and uninstall what you don't use. Free up space by clearing old photos, unused apps, and cache. A little regular tidying keeps your phone fast and functional.
