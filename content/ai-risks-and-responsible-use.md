## Card: What it is
image: responsibleAiHero
As AI grows more capable, it brings real risks alongside its benefits — from convincing fakes to privacy concerns and over-reliance. **Responsible use** means enjoying AI's advantages while staying aware of its dangers, both as a user and as someone affected by others' use of it.

## Card: Why it matters
AI can be misused to deceive, and even well-intentioned use has pitfalls. Being aware protects you from AI-powered scams and fakes, and helps you use AI in ways that are honest and healthy. This awareness is increasingly essential as AI-generated content floods everyday life.

## Card: Key terms
- **Deepfake**: realistic fake images, audio, or video made by AI.
- **Misinformation**: false information spread, sometimes via AI.
- **Over-reliance**: depending on AI so much that skills fade.
- **Privacy**: control over your personal information.

## Card: Fakes and misinformation
AI can now generate convincing fake images, voices, and videos — **deepfakes** — and floods of realistic but false text. This supercharges **misinformation** and scams: a "video" of someone, or a "voice call" from a relative, may be fake. The lesson: be more skeptical of digital media, and verify surprising or emotional content through other channels.

## Card: Privacy and over-reliance
image: aiPrivacyReliance
Two quieter risks. **Privacy**: AI tools may store what you type, so avoid sharing sensitive data. **Over-reliance**: leaning on AI for everything can erode your own skills and judgment — useful as a helper, risky as a crutch. Used thoughtfully, AI augments you; used carelessly, it can dull the very abilities it's meant to assist.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: believing images, audio, or video are real just because they look convincing; spreading AI-generated misinformation without checking; oversharing private data with AI tools; and outsourcing thinking entirely, losing your own skills. Responsible use means staying skeptical, protecting your data, and keeping your judgment engaged.

## Card: Quick recap
AI brings real risks: convincing deepfakes and misinformation, privacy concerns, and over-reliance. Be more skeptical of digital media and verify surprising content, keep sensitive data out of AI tools, and use AI as a helper rather than a crutch. Enjoy the benefits, but stay aware and keep your judgment switched on.
