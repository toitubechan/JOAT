## Card: What it is
image: growthGoalsWhyTheyMatterHero
A **goal** is a clear outcome you're working toward — something specific you want to achieve. Goals turn vague wishes ("I want to be healthier") into targets you can actually aim at and measure progress against. They give your effort direction, so you're moving *toward* something rather than just drifting.

## Card: Why it matters
Without goals, effort scatters and motivation fades — you can work hard yet go nowhere in particular. Clear goals focus your energy, help you decide what to do (and not do), and provide the satisfaction of progress. They're the difference between busy drifting and purposeful movement toward a life you want.

## Card: Key terms
- **Goal**: a specific outcome you're working toward.
- **Direction**: knowing which way you're heading.
- **Motivation**: the drive to pursue a goal.
- **Vague wish**: a desire too unclear to act on.

## Card: Goals give direction
image: growthGoalsWhyTheyMatterGoalsGiveDirectionHero
A goal acts like a destination on a map: it tells you which way to go and lets you tell whether you're getting closer. Without one, you might work hard but wander. With a clear goal, every choice can be measured against it — "does this move me toward my goal?" — turning scattered effort into focused **direction**.

## Card: From wish to goal
Most people have **vague wishes** — be fitter, save money, learn a skill — that never happen because they're too fuzzy to act on. A goal makes the wish concrete: what exactly, by when, and how you'll know you've reached it. That specificity is what transforms a daydream into something you can actually pursue and achieve.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: having only vague wishes instead of clear goals; setting goals so big and distant they feel hopeless; chasing too many goals at once, splitting focus; and setting goals based on what others expect rather than what *you* want, which kills motivation. A clear, personal, focused goal is what actually drives action.

## Card: Quick recap
A goal is a clear outcome you're working toward, turning vague wishes into targets you can aim at and measure. Goals give direction and focus your energy, making the difference between drifting and purposeful progress. Keep them specific, personal, and focused — that's what transforms a wish into real achievement.
