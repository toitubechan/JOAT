## Card: What it is
image: growthGoalsSmartGoalsHero
**SMART** is a popular framework for setting effective goals. It stands for **Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound**. Running a goal through these five checks turns a vague intention into a clear, actionable target — one you can actually pursue and know when you've reached.

## Card: Why it matters
Most goals fail because they're too vague to act on or track. The SMART framework fixes the most common weaknesses in one go. It's a quick, practical checklist that dramatically improves your odds of actually achieving what you set out to do, whether in fitness, work, money, or learning.

## Card: Key terms
- **Specific**: clearly defined, not vague.
- **Measurable**: you can track progress and know when it's done.
- **Achievable**: realistic given your situation.
- **Time-bound**: has a deadline.

## Card: Specific and measurable
image: growthGoalsSmartGoalsSpecificAndMeasurableHero
**Specific**: define exactly what you want — "run 5km without stopping," not "get fitter." **Measurable**: include a way to track progress and know you've succeeded — a number, a milestone. Vague goals can't be acted on or measured, so these two checks alone fix most weak goals by forcing clarity.

## Card: Achievable, relevant, time-bound
**Achievable**: ambitious but realistic for your situation — a goal you can't reach just demotivates. **Relevant**: it genuinely matters to *you* and your bigger picture, or you won't sustain it. **Time-bound**: set a deadline, since open-ended goals get postponed forever. A date creates gentle, useful pressure to start and continue.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common mistakes: vague goals with no measure ("be better with money"); goals with no deadline, so they're endlessly delayed; unrealistic goals that crush motivation when missed; and goals that aren't truly relevant to you. Running a goal through all five SMART checks catches each of these before they sink it.

## Card: Quick recap
SMART goals are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. The framework turns vague intentions into clear, trackable targets with deadlines, fixing the most common reasons goals fail. Run any goal through these five checks, and you'll know exactly what you're aiming at and when you've reached it.
