## Card: What it is
image: cuisineFrenchHero
**French cooking** is renowned for its emphasis on *technique* — refined methods and foundational skills that have profoundly influenced cuisines worldwide. More than specific dishes, French cuisine is famous for the *craft* of cooking: precise techniques, sauces, and a structured approach that forms the basis of much professional cooking. Understanding it reveals why French cooking is considered foundational.

## Card: Why it matters
French cooking has had an outsized influence on global and professional cuisine — many cooking techniques and the structure of professional kitchens come from the French tradition. Understanding it reveals the foundations of much of what's considered good cooking, and offers techniques that improve any cook's skills. It's foundational culinary knowledge with influence far beyond France.

## Card: Key terms
- **French cuisine**: the food tradition of France, famed for technique.
- **Technique**: refined cooking methods and skills.
- **Foundational influence**: French cooking's effect on global cuisine.
- **Sauces**: a celebrated element of French cooking.

## Card: A cuisine of technique
image: cuisineFrenchACuisineOfTechniqueHero
What sets **French cuisine** apart is its emphasis on **technique** — refined, precise methods of cooking developed and codified over centuries. French cooking is famous for things like its **sauces**, precise knife skills, and structured methods for preparing ingredients. It treats cooking as a craft to be mastered through technique, not just recipes. This focus on skill and method — *how* you cook, not just what — is the essence of the French culinary tradition.

## Card: Foundational influence
French cooking has had enormous **foundational influence** on global cuisine. Many cooking techniques taught and used worldwide, the structure and roles of professional kitchens, and much of the vocabulary of fine cooking come from the French tradition. This is why French cooking is often considered the foundation of professional culinary training. Learning French techniques benefits cooking of all kinds, because so much of "good cooking" technique traces back to or aligns with this tradition. Its influence reaches far beyond French dishes themselves.

## Card: Common mistakes
Common misunderstandings: thinking French cuisine is just fancy or pretentious dishes (its real significance is technique and foundational skills); focusing on specific dishes while missing the emphasis on *method*; not realizing how much global and professional cooking derives from French technique; and assuming it's irrelevant to everyday cooking (its techniques improve any cook). French cooking's essence and influence lie in technique — the craft of *how* to cook.

## Card: Quick recap
French cooking is renowned not just for dishes but for technique — refined, precise methods (like sauces and knife skills) developed over centuries, treating cooking as a craft to master. It has had enormous foundational influence: much of global and professional cooking technique, kitchen structure, and culinary vocabulary derives from the French tradition. This is why it's considered foundational, and why its techniques benefit cooks of all kinds.
