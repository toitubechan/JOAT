## Card: What it is
image: brainNervesHero
Your **brain** and **nervous system** are the body's control center and electrical wiring. The brain processes information and makes decisions; **nerves** carry messages at lightning speed between the brain and the rest of the body. Together they let you think, feel, move, and react — running both your conscious and automatic actions.

## Card: Why it matters
This system is *you* in a real sense — your thoughts, senses, memories, and movements all run through it. Understanding the basics demystifies how you perceive the world and react to it, and why sleep, stress, and habits affect your mind so much. It's the foundation of everything you experience.

## Card: Key terms
- **Brain**: the control center that processes and decides.
- **Nerves**: wiring carrying electrical messages around the body.
- **Neuron**: a single nerve cell.
- **Reflex**: an automatic, instant response bypassing conscious thought.

## Card: The control center and wiring
image: nervousSystemWiring
The **brain** receives information, makes sense of it, and sends out instructions. **Nerves**, made of cells called **neurons**, are the wiring that carries these electrical signals — from your senses *to* the brain, and from the brain *out* to muscles and organs. The spinal cord is the main cable linking brain to body.

## Card: Conscious vs. automatic
Some actions are conscious (deciding to pick something up); many are automatic (breathing, heartbeat, digestion) — handled without you thinking. **Reflexes** are the fastest: touch something hot and your hand pulls back *before* your brain consciously registers pain, because the signal takes a shortcut through the spinal cord to save time.

## Card: Common mistakes
A common misunderstanding is thinking the brain only handles "thinking," when it also runs automatic functions and processes every sensation. Another is underrating how much sleep and stress affect the brain's performance. And many assume the brain is fixed, when it can actually adapt and rewire — a property called plasticity.

## Card: Quick recap
The brain and nervous system are your control center and wiring: the brain processes and decides, while nerves (made of neurons) carry electrical messages throughout the body. Some actions are conscious, many automatic, and reflexes are near-instant shortcuts. It's the system behind everything you think, feel, and do.
