# Joat Account Deletion

Joat users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data
at any time.

## How to request deletion

Email **toitubechan@gmail.com** from the email address linked to your Joat
account with the subject **Joat account deletion request**.

Include only the email address used for your Joat account. Do not send your
password, verification code, payment information, or other sensitive data.

We may ask you to verify that you control the account before processing the
request. After verification, we will request deletion of the account data held
by our authentication provider and any other personal data we control, subject
to any information we must retain for security, fraud prevention, legal, or
financial record-keeping obligations.

Learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. You can
remove that local data by clearing Joat's app data in Android settings or by
uninstalling the app.

## Questions

For questions about account or data deletion, contact
**toitubechan@gmail.com**.
